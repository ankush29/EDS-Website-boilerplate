About WKND

We believe weekends are made for mountains, oceans, and everything in between.

Our Mission

WKND Magazine was founded in 2012 with a single conviction: the world is best experienced with your boots on the ground, your paddle in the water, or your tyres on a dirt road.

We document the places and pursuits that remind us what it means to be alive — not through the lens of luxury travel, but through authentic adventure available to anyone with curiosity and a free weekend.

Every article, photo essay, and trip guide is crafted by people who've been there, done that, and can't wait to go back.

What We Stand For

Authenticity

Every story we tell is firsthand. No press trips, no sponsored illusions — just real people and real experiences.

Accessibility

Adventure isn't reserved for the elite. We champion experiences at every budget, fitness level, and time constraint.

Responsibility

We only have one planet. WKND actively promotes Leave No Trace principles, low-impact travel, and conservation.

Our Story

2012 — Founded

Three friends return from a surf trip in Costa Rica, convinced the adventure they experienced deserves to be shared. WKND launches as a photojournal blog.

2015 — Print Edition

Growing readership leads to the launch of the quarterly print edition. The inaugural issue sells out in 48 hours.

2019 — Adventure Trips

WKND begins running small-group guided expeditions, taking readers to the places they've been reading about for years.

2024 — AEM Digital Platform

WKND relaunches on Adobe Experience Manager Edge Delivery Services, delivering 100/100 Lighthouse scores and blazing-fast pages worldwide.
