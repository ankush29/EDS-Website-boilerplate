Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about WKND Magazine.

Got questions? We have answers.

Whether you're a long-time reader or discovering WKND for the first time, here are answers to the questions we hear most often. Can't find what you're looking for? Drop us a line.

General

What is WKND Magazine?

WKND Magazine is an adventure and outdoor lifestyle publication dedicated to inspiring readers to explore the world beyond the ordinary. We cover surfing, cycling, hiking, road trips, and any pursuit that gets you off the couch and into the wild.

How often is WKND published?

We publish new articles every week on our digital platform. Our print edition releases quarterly, with special collector editions for major expeditions and events.

How do I subscribe to WKND?

Digital access is free — just bookmark wknd.site and visit whenever inspiration strikes. For the premium print + digital package, visit our subscription page where you can choose from monthly, annual, or lifetime plans.

Content & Submissions

Can I submit my adventure story to WKND?

Absolutely! We love discovering new voices. Send a brief pitch (250 words max) along with two or three example photos to pitch@wknd.site. We review all submissions and respond within three weeks.

Does WKND accept freelance photography?

Yes. We work with freelance photographers regularly. Please send your portfolio and a brief introduction to photo@wknd.site. We're especially interested in authentic, unposed adventure and travel photography.

Can I republish WKND content on my blog or website?

Our content is copyright protected. For syndication requests, licensed use, or quoting guidelines, contact rights@wknd.site. Short excerpts with a clear attribution link are generally acceptable for editorial contexts.

Adventures & Trips

Does WKND organise guided adventure trips?

Twice a year WKND partners with expert local guides to run small-group adventure trips to locations featured in the magazine. Spaces are limited and sell out quickly. Sign up to our newsletter to be the first to hear about upcoming trips.

What fitness level is required for WKND trips?

Each trip has a clear fitness rating from 1 (relaxed) to 5 (serious athlete). Most of our trips sit at 3 — active and challenging, but achievable for anyone who exercises regularly. Full details are always included on the trip listing page.

What is the refund policy for WKND trips?

Full refund up to 60 days before departure. 50% refund between 30 and 60 days. No refund within 30 days, though you are welcome to transfer your place to another participant. We strongly recommend travel insurance.
